# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Give and receive constructive feedback professionally.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Handle ecological, geographic, and community-sensitive data with care.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, insults, or personal attacks.
- Public or private disclosure of sensitive information without permission.
- Publishing private occurrence data, credentials, or identifying information.
- Sustained disruption of discussions, issues, or reviews.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the project or community. Serious concerns can be reported through the project's security or maintainer contact channels. Maintainers should review reports promptly, fairly, and with appropriate confidentiality.

This code is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
